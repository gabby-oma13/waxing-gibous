LEARN ABOUT CAPYBARAS!

Description

Capybaras are the world's largest rodent known for their semi-aquatic lifestyle, friendly nature, and unique diet!

They are excellent swimmers and can hold their breath for up to 5 minutes!

FUN FACTS!!

1. Did you know that capybaras have exellent vocalisation and communicate through whistling and barking?

2. Did you know that capybaras teeth never stop growing !

3. Did you know that they are so fluffy that other animals use them as pillows?
